Sithma's Cakes & Creations Academy

Introduction to baking is the perfect place to start your cake journey; providing you with all the basic skills, knowledge and confidence for you to create wonderful cakes every time. As a classically trained chef, sithma will teach you the professional techniques needed to bake, level, split and fill your cakes. Using only the finest ingredients you will learn all the essential skills needed to create a delicious sponge cake, how to make our scrumptious buttercream, and how cover a single tier cake. You will learn everything you need to know to make a professional single tier cake that is also suitable for stacking as part of a tiered cake, and you will have a delicious creation of your own to take home

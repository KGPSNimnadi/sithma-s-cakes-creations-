ABOUT US

We are Sithma's cakes & creations academy

In the past Sithma’s Cakes & Creations was a small self-employed business. It was started on March 24, 2022, in Kuliyapitiya.
The owner of this Sithma’s Cakes & Creations is Miss.Pawani Sithma Originally Sithma’s Cakes & Creations was a small homemade cake shop.
It has grown little by little today. They are getting many cake orders today.
They can make delicious birthday cakes, anniversary cakes, wedding cakes, gateau cakes, etc.
On May 06, 2022, she started her Sithma’s Cakes & Creations Academy.
The Sithma’s Cakes & Creations Academy is the best place to follow a cake-baking & decorating course in the Kuliyapitiya area. It is a two months course.
They are awarded a certificate after completing the course. Now there are many students learning cake-baking and decorating methods with Miss.Pawani Sithma.
The teacher of this academy is very friendly so they are learning together. All the students are like a family.
